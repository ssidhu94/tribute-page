Kobe Bean Bryant

One of the greatest NBA players of our generation

Here is a timeline of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born August 23 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Graduated from Lower Merion High School in Ardmore, located in the Philadelphia subrub of Lower Merion
  • 1996 - Drafted the 13th pick by the Charlotte Hornets and subsequently traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 1997 - Became the All Star Dunk Contest, becoming the youngest dunk champion at the age of 18, and earned a spot on the NBA All-Rookie Second Team
  • 2000 - Won first of three championships in a row, cementing his first Three-Peat
  • 2002 - Earned first NBA All-Star MVP trophy
  • 2003 - Bryant's fallout with Shaquille O'Neal lead to O'Neal to be traded and ending a dynamic duo
    • 2006 - Scored a career-high 81 points in a victory against the Toronto Raptors 2006 - To begin the 2006-2007 season, Bryant changed his jersey number from 8 to 24
  • 2008 - Kobe was awarded the season MVP award for his tremeandous season
  • 2009 - Defeating the Orlando Magic in five games, Bryant was awarded his first NBA Finals MVP trophy upon winning his fourth championship
  • 2010 - Defeating the Boston Celtics in seven games, Bryant was awarded his second consecutive NBA Finals MVP award and his fifth championship
  • 2013 - Bryant's last remaining playing years were unfortunately plauged by injuries and poor team performances
  • 2016 - Bryant retires from the NBA after finishing his final game against the Utah Jazz scoring 60 points against the Utah Jazz
  • 2020 - Kobe Bryant was involved in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California and passed away on January 26
